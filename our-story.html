Our Story

Made fresh, made with love

Blueberry Cape May gives a variety of French Crêpes: Sweet and Savory. Served all day long. We also make Fresh Fruit Juices and Smoothies, Nutella Milkshakes and Boba.

“It began 4 years ago when we started for the first time serving Cape May County and we had been very successful since then. Eat healthy, live longer!” — Chef Ahhmed, the owner

Whether you're stopping by for a savory crêpe at lunch, an afternoon boba, or a late-night milkshake, we're honored to be part of your day. Thank you for making Blueberry Cape May your spot.
