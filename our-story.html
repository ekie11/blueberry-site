Our Story

Made fresh, made with love

Blueberry began with a simple idea: bring together the delicate art of the French crêpe and the playful joy of fresh boba tea under one roof. What started as a small griddle and a big dream has grown into a place where neighbors gather, friends catch up, and every visit ends with something sweet.

Everything on our menu is made to order. Our crêpe batter is prepared fresh each morning, our smoothies and juices are blended from real fruit, and our boba is shaken by hand — never from a machine. From the signature Dubai Chocolate crêpe with pistachio to a classic Lemon Butter & Sugar, we put the same care into every plate and every cup.

Whether you're stopping by for a savory crêpe at lunch, an afternoon boba, or a late-night milkshake, we're honored to be part of your day. Thank you for making Blueberry your spot.
